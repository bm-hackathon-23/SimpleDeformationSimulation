Brain deformation simulation for the Brain/MINDS Hackathon 2023.

Using the Brain/MINDS 3D Marmoset Reference Brain Atlas 2019 segmentation for the left hemisphere of the cortex region (Yellow) a simple 2D grid model was generated.

The one shown on the left is one slice of the volume, this data is shown as a grayscale image, black pixels are not considered for the simulation.

Full 3D volume of the region including boundary and volume was generated from this data, but a 3D Hybrid SPH and elastic simulation was not achieved during hackathon.
